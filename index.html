About The Green Classroom

The Green Classroom is a research initiative dedicated to mapping and analyzing Pakistan’s agricultural supply chains. We provide critical insights into how markets, policies, and power relations shape food systems, with the mission to make agriculture more sustainable, inclusive, and equitable. By bridging research and practice, we aim to strengthen farmer livelihoods and enhance food security across Pakistan.
