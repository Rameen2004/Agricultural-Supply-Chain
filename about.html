About the Project

Our comprehensive research initiative focuses on mapping and analyzing Pakistan's agricultural supply chains to enhance food security, improve market efficiency, and support sustainable agricultural development.

Our Mission

We aim to create a comprehensive digital mapping system that visualizes the complex network of agricultural supply chains across Pakistan. This project addresses critical gaps in agricultural data and provides stakeholders with actionable insights for policy-making and market optimization.

Through advanced data collection, analysis, and visualization techniques, we're building a platform that connects farmers, distributors, retailers, and policymakers in an integrated ecosystem.

Food Security

Enhancing food security through better supply chain visibility and management.

Market Efficiency

Improving market efficiency by connecting producers directly with consumers.

Policy Support

Supporting evidence-based policy decisions through comprehensive data insights.

How It Began

The genesis of our agricultural supply chain mapping project stems from a critical need to understand and optimize Pakistan's complex agricultural ecosystem.

The Challenge

Pakistan's agricultural sector contributes significantly to the national economy, yet lacks comprehensive mapping of its supply chains. This gap creates inefficiencies, information asymmetries, and barriers to sustainable development.

Our research team identified the urgent need for a systematic approach to document, analyze, and visualize these complex networks that connect millions of farmers to consumers across the country.

  • Identified market inefficiencies
  • Recognized data gaps in agricultural sector
  • Established research methodology

Research Inception

The project began in early 2023 when our multidisciplinary team of agricultural economists, data scientists, and policy researchers came together to address this critical knowledge gap.

Field Implementation

We developed innovative data collection methodologies and deployed field teams across multiple provinces to gather comprehensive supply chain information from farmers to end consumers.
